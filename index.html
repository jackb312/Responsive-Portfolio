About Me

I'm Jack Brettell, and thank you for taking the time to check out my portfolio. Coding and development is my second career, as I got into it after many years in wine and spirits sales. My passion is helping my clients achieve what they want to convey in web, mobile and gaming platforms. I have many years of experience and many positive reviews of my work. Please navigate to the portfolio page to view some of the work I've done for my clients. All inquiries can be sent by email or by phone on the contact page. Look forward to working with you soon!

I'm originally from Kansas City. I graduated from Kansas State University in 2010 with a degree in Political Philosophy. I moved to Seattle in 2016. I decided to make the transition to coding and development because I wanted to learn a skillset that would be valuable for many years to come. Before embarking in the transition, I did some self studying through codecademy, and discovered I enjoyed the challenge of working with code. In my spare time I enjoy running, climbing, sailing, and exploring the beautiful Pacific Northwest.
